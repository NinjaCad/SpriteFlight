Work in Progress

Instructions

Press 1, 2, or 3 to pick a character.
Use the arrow keys to make the player move.
Press the space bar when you finish moving the character.
Press 1, 2, or 3 to pick another character.
Watch as your last character has saved it's movements.
Stand on the red button to open doors.
Stand on green button to move on to the next level.
